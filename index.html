Projects

Innovation

Autonomus Car Fleet Administration Efficiency

Improving efficiency through data sourcing

This Project was developed under the premise that in a few years time, every major car company is going to have autonomus driving technology and that the main source of profit will be an autonomus car sharing membership. The key will be how to administer that fleet in the most efficient way possible. To this end, the seemingly unrelated calendar app was completly overhauled to a web based service for both consumers an businesses to handle all schedules and gather data to aticipate the needs for logistics and transportation.

Communication

Veganism Communication

Changing society's perception of Veganism

In this project, I created a communication campaign for a ‘MOLE’, a company that develops vegan foods. The objective of the campaign is to shift the negative perception the majority of society has of veganism, into an aspirational and desirable. And in the process, undo years of poor, aggressive and antagonizing communication from activist groups and sentiment forming lobbying from interest groups such as the meat and dairy industries.

Innovation

Material and Product Innovation

Development of recycled rubber materials and products

Looking to find a solution for the problem of certain materials I developed for this project a “sintered recycled rubber” material sourced mainly from old tiers. The main thesis for the project was that the discarded tiers where a wasted opportunity but also that this new material, though recycled from waste, afforded certain new esthetical qualities that could be applied to a wide range of high end products, where this material had never been used before.

Industrial Design

Rapid Construction Infrastructure

Development of rapid construction structures and infrastructure for catastrophe situations

The “Blop” rapid construction system is a conceptual development for a catastrophe like situations where a large group of people needs to be relocated with urgency. It consist of a collection of interconnected inflatable structures that are filled with expandable bio-plastics, which later become rigid structures in 24 hours.

Product Design

CMYK Bench

Conceptual bench development for IKEA

CMYK is a simple conceptual product developed for Ikea. The objective of the design was to create a bench in keeping with the brands ethos in terms of design, materials, etc. but with a layout that allowed for the packaging to be a product on itself. The packaging showcases the product in such a way that it becomes a desirable product on its own while, at the same time, reducing costs and materials used in traditional packaging.
